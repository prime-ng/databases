# Transport Reports & Advanced Analytics – Consolidated Framework

## Objective
This document defines an enterprise-grade, consolidated Transport Reporting Framework for a School ERP.
It replaces fragmented, overlapping reports with dimension-driven, scalable core reports.

---

## Design Principles
- One report per business fact
- Filters replace duplicate reports
- Charts replace summary-only reports
- Drilldowns replace stop-wise / class-wise reports
- BI & AI ready by design

---

## Consolidated Core Reports

### 1. Route Performance Report
Covers route configuration, utilization, delays, stop density.

### 2. Student Transport Usage Report
Covers allocation, attendance consistency, pickup/drop behavior.

### 3. Stop & Locality Analysis Report
Covers stop-level demand and congestion patterns.

### 4. Vehicle Efficiency & Utilization Report
Covers fleet capacity usage and cost efficiency.

### 5. Driver & Attendant Performance Report
Covers staff attendance, reliability, and trip handling.

### 6. Trip Execution & Discipline Report
Covers planned vs actual trips and delays.

### 7. Transport Finance & Leakage Report
Covers fee assignment, collection, and misuse.

### 8. Cost & Maintenance Analytics Report
Covers fuel, maintenance, breakdown trends.

### 9. Management Summary Dashboard
Aggregated KPIs only (non-operational).

---

## Final Outcome
- Reduced reports from ~30 to 9
- Cleaner menu structure
- Faster queries
- BI / AI readiness
