  2. minifyHtml() private helper

  Strips HTML comments and collapses multi-space whitespace between tags before handing HTML to DomPDF. Blade templates with lots of @if/@foreach blocks produce HTML with significant whitespace. Smaller DOM
   = faster parsing.

  3. Organization::first() moved outside the per-student loop

  Was being re-queried for every student in generateReportPdf(). Now loaded once before the loop.

  ---
  Important note on isRemoteEnabled => false: your PDF Blade templates must not use asset() URLs (which generate http://... URLs). They must use public_path() to produce absolute filesystem paths instead.
  The third_pdf and fourth_pdf templates already follow this pattern — double-check first_pdf and second_pdf if those also have slow generation.
