Here's a comprehensive list of all possible roles staff may have in a school in India, organized by department and function:
Academic Leadership
— Principal, Vice Principal, Academic Director, Academic Coordinator, Head of Department (HOD), Dean of Studies, Examination Controller
Teaching Staff
— PGT (Post Graduate Teacher), TGT (Trained Graduate Teacher), PRT (Primary Teacher), NTT (Nursery Trained Teacher), Guest/Visiting Faculty, Subject Teacher, Lab Demonstrator, Workshop Instructor, Special Educator (CWSN/Inclusive Education), Remedial Teacher, Language Teacher (Hindi, English, Sanskrit, Regional), Physical Education Teacher (PET), Art & Craft Teacher, Music Teacher, Dance Teacher, Yoga Teacher, Computer Teacher/IT Instructor, Home Science Teacher, Work Experience Teacher
Student Support & Counseling
— School Counselor/Psychologist, Career Guidance Counselor, Student Welfare Officer, Health & Wellness Teacher (under NEP 2020), Anti-Bullying Coordinator, Child Protection Officer, Special Needs Coordinator (SENCO)
Administrative Staff
— School Administrator, Office Superintendent, Head Clerk, UDC (Upper Division Clerk), LDC (Lower Division Clerk), Data Entry Operator, Receptionist/Front Desk Officer, PRO (Public Relations Officer), Admissions Officer/Coordinator, Fee Clerk/Cashier, Store Keeper/Inventory Manager, Record Keeper/Archivist
Finance & Accounts
— Accountant/Bursar, Finance Officer, Accounts Clerk, Audit Assistant, Payroll Officer, Tax & Compliance Officer
Examination Cell
— Examination Controller, Exam Coordinator, Result Analyst, Question Paper Setter, Exam Invigilator/Supervisor
Library
— Librarian, Assistant Librarian, Library Attendant, Digital Resource Manager
Laboratory
— Lab Assistant (Physics/Chemistry/Biology), Lab Attendant/Peon, Computer Lab In-charge, Language Lab Coordinator
Information Technology
— IT Administrator/System Admin, Network Administrator, ERP Administrator, Website/Social Media Manager, AV/Smart Classroom Technician, CCTV & Security Systems Operator
Transport
— Transport Manager/In-charge, Fleet Supervisor, Bus Driver, Bus Conductor, Bus Attendant/Aaya (for junior students), Route Planner, GPS Tracking Operator
Hostel & Residential
— Hostel Warden (Boys/Girls), Assistant Warden, House Master/House Mistress, Matron, Hostel Supervisor, Mess Manager/Caterer, Cook/Chef, Helper/Kitchen Staff
Health & Medical
— School Doctor/Medical Officer, School Nurse, First Aid Attendant, Visiting Specialist (Dentist, Eye Specialist), Nutrition/Dietitian Consultant
Security
— Security In-charge, Security Guard/Watchman, Gate Keeper, Fire Safety Officer
Sports & Co-curricular
— Sports Coach/Trainer (Cricket, Football, Basketball, Athletics, etc.), Swimming Instructor, NCC Officer/Caretaker, Scout & Guide Master, Eco Club/Science Club/Literary Club Coordinator, Event Manager/Cultural Coordinator
Maintenance & Facilities
— Estate Manager/Facility Manager, Electrician, Plumber, Carpenter, Gardener/Mali, Sweeper/Safai Karmachari, Peon/Attendant/Class IV Staff, Generator Operator, Water Purifier/RO Technician, AC/HVAC Technician, Painter
Housekeeping & Support
— Housekeeping Supervisor, Aaya/Caretaker (for pre-primary), Washroom Attendant, Laundry Staff (in boarding schools)
Management & Governance
— Managing Director/Chairman, Secretary (Society/Trust), Manager (as per RTE/State rules), Treasurer, Board/Committee Member
Compliance & Legal
— Compliance Officer, RTI Coordinator, Legal Advisor, POCSO Compliance Officer, RTE Nodal Officer, Grievance Redressal Officer, Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) Member (under POSH Act)
NEP 2020 & Modern Roles
— Foundational Literacy & Numeracy (FLN) Coordinator, Competency-Based Education Coordinator, Skill Development Instructor (vocational subjects), Coding & Robotics Instructor, AI/STEM Lab Facilitator, Experiential Learning Coordinator, Bagless Day Coordinator, Mentor Teacher, Teacher Trainer/Professional Development Coordinator
Outsourced/Contractual Roles
— Canteen Manager/Vendor, School Photographer/Videographer, Uniform & Stationery Vendor Coordinator, Annual Day/Event Contractor Liaison, School Bus Vendor Coordinator, Pest Control Coordinator
This covers the full spectrum — from CBSE/ICSE/State Board private schools to government schools, residential/boarding schools, and institutions aligned with NEP 2020 norms. The exact titles vary by state regulations, board affiliation, and school size.
