# Unique Features of the Application

### Permission Management
  - School can define and configure different permissions levels for different roles.
  - Permission can assigned upto multi level (Menu level/Page Level/ Tab Level/Button level/Action Level)
  - Permission can be assigned differently to the different roles (e.g. School Admin, Class Teacher, Subject Teacher, Student)
  - Permission can be assigned differently to the different users/Group/Role/Department/Designation/Entity Groups.

### Exam Grading & Division
  - School may have different Grading & Division system in different classes (For Class Nursary to 2 Grading will be different then From Class 3-5). May be Class 6-8 will have different Grading & Division system then class 9-12th.

### Analytics on Student Performance
  - Same way Analytics will be different for different classes.Different Categories for Junior Class then Senior Classes.
  - Analytics will be upto 6-8 Level deep on Every Subject (Lesson, Topic, Sub-Topic, Mini Topic, Sub-Mini Topic, Micro Topic, Sub-Micro Topic, Nano Topic, Ultra Topic)

### Transport Management
  - Our Application Support Single Side Transport Facility also. School can Allow or Restrict Single Side Transport Facility.
  - Our App. support Pickup and Drop from Different Stops. Fees will be calculated accordingly.
